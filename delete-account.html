Delete Account & Data

If you wish to stop using the GNE Home Automation service, you can request the complete and permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data.

What data will be permanently removed?
  • Your profile credentials (Name, Email, and Authentication tokens).
  • All linked IoT hardware configurations and custom board names.
  • Your automation routines, timer presets, and historical sensor data.

How to Request Deletion:

Option 1 (In-App): If you still have the app installed, simply navigate to Profile > Account Settings and click Delete Account for instant automatic deletion.

Option 2 (Web Request): If you have already uninstalled the app, click the button below to email our technical support team. Please send the email from the exact address used to register your smart home account. We will process your deletion request and purge your database entries within 48 hours.
